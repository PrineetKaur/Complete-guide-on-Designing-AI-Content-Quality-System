# Examples

This document shows real input/output examples using the tone consistency system.

---

## Example 1: Error Message

**Input:**
Your request has not been processed due to an unexpected error.

**Output:**
- Clarity: 3/5
- Tone: 2/5
- Conciseness: 3/5
- Actionability: 1/5

**Issues:**
- Overly formal
- No guidance
- Slightly verbose

**Improved Version:**
Something went wrong. Please try again.

---

## Example 2: Payment Failure

**Input:**
Transaction unsuccessful. Please verify your payment credentials and attempt again.

**Output:**
- Clarity: 3/5
- Tone: 2/5
- Conciseness: 2/5
- Actionability: 3/5

**Issues:**
- Too formal
- Slightly complex wording

**Improved Version:**
Payment failed. Check your details or try another method.

## Example 4: Overly Casual Tone

**Input:**
Oops! We totally messed up 😅

**Output:**
- Clarity: 3/5
- Tone: 1/5
- Conciseness: 4/5
- Actionability: 1/5

**Issues:**
- Too casual
- Not reassuring
- No next step

**Improved Version:**
Something went wrong. Please try again.

---

## Example 5: Success Message

**Input:**
Your request has been successfully processed.

**Output:**
- Clarity: 4/5
- Tone: 3/5
- Conciseness: 3/5
- Actionability: 2/5

**Issues:**
- Slightly formal
- Could be more natural

**Improved Version:**
You're all set!

---

## Key Takeaway

Consistent improvements follow predictable patterns:
- simplify language  
- reduce formality  
- add guidance  
- align tone
